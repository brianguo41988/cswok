About Us

Dating back to Chapel Hill, NC, three young computer science students were inspired by Asian restaurants such as Panda Express and Pei Wei. Starting from a small local business, CSWok has grown to become a global phenomenon that has taken millenials by storm. Our dishes are highly rated, receiving phrases from local communities to well known celebrities like Nicki Minaj and Ryan Reynolds. Additionally, all our ingredients are locally grown and dishes made fresh everyday!
